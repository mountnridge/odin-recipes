Loco Moco Recipe

Description

A loco moco is a local Hawaiian dish served at a variety of drive ins.

Ingredients

  • 2 scoops of rice
  • 2 over easy eggs
  • 2 hamgurger patties
  • gravy

Steps

  1. Start rice using a rice cooker
  2. Prepare hamburger patty to your preferred donenss
  3. Cook 2 over easy eggs
  4. Prepare gravy according to package directions
  5. Put 2 scoops of rice on plate, top with 2 hamburger patties, smother with gravy and top with 2 eggs.
