Mai Tai Recipe

Description

Tropical rum drink that is as complex as it is fruity. Perfect for a sunset on the beach or poolside!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz aged rum
  • 0.5 oz orange liqueur
  • 0.75 oz lime juice
  • 0.5 oz dark rum
  • pineapple slice for garnish

Steps

  1. Place the aged rum, orange liqueur, and lime juice in a cocktail shaker. Add ice and shake until cold.
  2. Strain the drink into an ice-filled glass. Top with the dark rum. Garnish with pineapple slice
